CASE STUDIES

Multiple Industries and Business Models

At Fogatel, we’ve partnered with deeptech companies across a broad spectrum of industries—including consumer electronics, displays, mobile devices, automotive, aeronautics, space, and medtech.

Our experience spans various business models, such as IP licensing, fabless semiconductor operations, and full-stack product commercialization.

We’ve helped bring to market technologies ranging from advanced materials and film-based solutions to sensors, photonics, embedded systems, robotics, and fully integrated hardware products. No matter the domain, our team tailors each engagement to accelerate our clients’ success in the North American market.

Here are a few examples of client engagements

Smart Surfaces with Touch-Sensitive Wood

A European startup developed a sustainable, transparent, and touch-sensitive wood with wide applications in smart surfaces—from automotive to luxury interiors.

Fogatel defined a go-to-market strategy and led business development efforts with top CE OEMs including Bose, Microsoft, Amazon, Google, Apple, Facebook, Sonos, and Tesla—opening doors to key partnerships and pilot projects.

Multi-Touch Tech for Consumer Electronics

A French company developed an innovative touchscreen solution for consumer electronics. Fogatel led North American business development and sales, licensing the technology to a semiconductor vendor and a tier-one OEM, and introducing it to leading brands like Google, Microsoft, Dell, and Samsung.

Flexible Silver Nanowire Electrodes

A company developed a scalable manufacturing process for silver nanowires, enabling the production of transparent and flexible electrodes for next-generation touchscreens, OLEDs, and OPV devices.

Fogatel supported sales growth in emerging applications, leading to multi-million-dollar revenue and over 10 design wins with major OEMs including 3M, Lenovo, HP, Dell, Google, Amazon, and Microsoft.

micro-LED Technology

A company pioneering scalable micro-LED displays that integrate high-luminance LEDs with silicon transistors on a single wafer using standard semiconductor processes.

Fogatel led business development efforts. We secured and negotiated a development and licensing agreement with a leading global display manufacturer, positioning the company for commercial success.

Touch-Sensitive Robotics

A company developing next-generation sensory technology that enables machines to feel touch—paving the way for seamless collaboration between humans and robots.

Fogatel helped redefine the go-to-market strategy, focusing the product offering on a specific high-impact application.

Secure Temperature Sensing Technology

Fogatel supported a UK startup developing the world’s first secure temperature sensor, combining cybersecurity and sensing in a single chip. We crafted their go-to-market strategy and facilitated introductions to strategic partners in semiconductors, IoT, and industrial automation.
